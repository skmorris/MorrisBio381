I dont have good datasets for this, so I will use one of the built in datasets. Ive chosen the msleep dataset, which contains sleep data for mammals.

variables in the dataset:

  • name = common name
  • genus
  • vore = carnivore/omnivore/herbivore
  • order
  • conservation = conservation status of the animal
  • sleep_total = total sleep, in hours
  • sleep_rem = rem sleep, in hours
  • sleep_cycle = length of slep cycle, in hours
  • awake = amount of time spent awake, in hours
  • brainwt = brain weight in kilograms
  • bodywt = body weight in kilograms
